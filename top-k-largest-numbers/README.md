544. Top k Largest Numbers
lintcode, medium
English
Given an integer array, find the top k largest numbers in it.

Have you met this question in a real interview?  
Example
Example1

Input: [3, 10, 1000, -99, 4, 100] and k = 3
Output: [1000, 100, 10]
Example2

Input: [8, 7, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2, 1] and k = 5
Output: [8, 7, 6, 5, 4]
